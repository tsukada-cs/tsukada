Research Theme

Keywords：Typhoon, Hurricane, Satellite, Intensity estimation, Mesovortices

We developed a new method to estimate the lower-level winds in the "eye" of the typhoon/hurricane from geostationary meteorological satellites, and applied it to the Typhoon Lan (2017). In addition, we quantified the vorticity of the mesovortices and the rotation speed of "striations" inside the eyewall, which were visualized as asymmetric motions.
